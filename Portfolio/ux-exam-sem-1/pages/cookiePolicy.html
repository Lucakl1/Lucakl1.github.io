Cookie Policy for Trendsetting

Last updated: December 02 2025

Trendsetting uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, personalize content, and analyze website traffic. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies we use and how you can control them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help improve functionality and performance.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function, such as:

  • Shopping cart functionality
  • Page navigation
  • Secure login sessions

2.2 Performance & Analytics Cookies

These help us understand how visitors use the website, allowing us to improve it.

2.3 Functional Cookies

Used to remember user preferences, such as:

  • Language settings
  • Saved items
  • Login information

2.4 Marketing Cookies

Used to deliver personalized ads and track campaign performance.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use cookies from:

  • Google Analytics
  • Payment providers
  • Social media integrations

These third-party cookies follow their own privacy policies.

4. How You Can Manage Cookies

You can:

  • Adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies through our cookie banner
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Note: disabling cookies may affect website functionality.
